What it does?

⮞ The report after the MRI Scan takes a lot of time to return to the patient, which results into nothing but just tention. Sometimes during an emergency, this time gap can be fatal and usually people become very anxious while waiting for the report.

⮞ Through this web application they can easily check for the predicted results calculated by our trained model for Brain Tumor Detection.

STEP 1

Choose & Upload Image

Choose your MRI Scanned image from your device and click Upload.

STEP 2

Model checks for Tumor

The trained model runs the calculations and checks for the tumor

STEP 3

Display Results

Displays the Accuracy of the Tumor Detection
